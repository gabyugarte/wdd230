Our History

Bountiful fruits has a rich history dating back to the early 2000s, when its founders began experimenting with different fruit blends in their kitchen. Over the years, the company has grown in popularity, expanding its product line to include a variety of delicious and healthy juices, smoothies, and other beverages. The company is based in the bustling city center of downtown, where its modern facility is equipped with state-of-the-art equipment to ensure the highest quality products.

Meet The Team

Welcome to our "Meet the Team" page! Our company is made up of a talented and passionate group of individuals who are dedicated to providing top-notch service to our clients. We believe in transparency and building strong relationships with our customers, and we think that starts with getting to know the people behind the brand.

Kevin Ugarte

CEO & Founder

Kevin has over 20 years of experience in the food industry and holds a degree in business from Stanford University. He founded Bountiful Foods in 2010 with the mission of bringing healthy and sustainably-grown food to the local community. When he's not working on growing the business, you'll find him hiking in the nearby mountains or spending time with his family.

kevin.u@bountifulfoods.com

Mikela Ross

Chef - Natural food specialist

Mikela has been in the food industry for over a decade and is passionate about creating delicious and healthy meals using only natural ingredients. She trained at the Culinary Institute of America and has worked in some of the country's top restaurants. At Bountiful Foods, she oversees menu planning and recipe development. She also loves practicing yoga and running.

mikela.r@bountifulfoods.com

Johana Dowson

Nutritionist

Johana holds a master's degree in nutrition from the University of California, Berkeley and has a deep understanding of the relationship between food and health. She works closely with our team to ensure that all of our meals are well-balanced and meet our customers' nutritional needs. When she's not consulting with clients, you'll find her hiking or reading.
